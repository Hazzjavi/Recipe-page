Simple Omelette Recpie

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfect,optionally filled with your choice of chess, vegetable, or meats.

Perparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Perparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: Chesses,diced vegetable,cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the egg with a pinch of salt and pepper untill they are
    well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of a water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Add filling (optional):When the egg begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette.
  4. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the filling. Let it cook for another minutes, then slide it onto a plates
  5. Enjoy:server hot, withaddtional salt and pepper if needed
